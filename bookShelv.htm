Bookcases and shelving units for every room

Whether you are a book lover or just a collector of memorabilia, everybody has some books, photo frames, small sculptures, and all sorts of knick-knacks around the home. But all these things need a place to keep your lovely home neat and tidy. You also want to show these things off to your friends and family. IKEA can help with this with its wide selection of bookcases and shelving units.

You will find shelving units, bookcases, storage shelves and units, wall shelves, and pantry shelves by IKEA. These shelves come in different colours, styles, and designs for you to pick from. They can be placed in any room such as a bedroom, office room, bathroom, kitchen, or living room. You won't be disappointed.

Why should you buy a bookshelf?

When you are setting up your house, there are several pieces of furniture you have to deal with. A bookshelf is a perfect addition to your home. These are multipurpose since you can put books, small plants, small baubles, picture frames, and other small items on the shelves.

Bookshelves help greatly with the organisation of your belongings and your home. Books of all shapes and sizes can be placed on these shelves. Fat, slim, tall, short, standalone, multi-book series, or any other type of book can be accommodated. Items like picture frames, decorations, or special items can be showcased easily.

Bookshelves come in different sizes and shapes so you can pick which one suits your home the most. You can buy a wooden bookshelf or a metal rack for books. Either one will look perfect in your home based on your home's unique decor style. Bookshelves offer easy access to your books and other precious things and give people a peek into your personality. Bookshelves are practical and wonderful pieces of furniture.

Where should bookshelves go in your house?

You found a set of bookshelves for your new home. Wonderful. Now where do you place them? Bookshelves are versatile furniture pieces. You can place them in your living room and put several books on their shelves. This way, your guests can also browse through your collection when they are visiting you. Books can always be great conversation starters too. Your friends can easily take a book to read and your family members will be able to sit down on the sofa to comfortably read books picked up from your bookshelf for living room.

In the bedroom, your most favourite and treasured books and items will be close to you. You will be able to re-read those books any number of times you want. See your favourite moments captured in photos and reminisce. Place holiday photographs or childhood memories around you and sleep with these wonderful memories once they are neatly arranged on these shelves.

In your office room, you can keep all your books and files related to your work. Even in a paperless world, some hard copies are needed so all of us have storage space. As you sit at your office desk, the books and files will be within easy reach. This way you can focus more time on working and less time on looking for information.

For your bathroom, there are many bathroom accessories that are needed. Accessories include hand towels, bath towels, shampoo, conditioner, soaps, body wash, and so much more. All these things have their particular place in your bathroom. A stand will help you organise all these necessary items in their proper location.

A kitchen is an extremely important room in any house. All sorts of delicious food get made which you and your family will enjoy. Having a well organised kitchen makes life so much fun. A pantry stand will keep the ingredients and condiments sorted. Otherwise, you will be running around looking for things instead of focusing on cooking mouthwatering dishes.

What should you look for in a bookshelf?

Since there are so many different types and shapes of bookshelves, everybody can get their particular type for their homes. The first thing is to decide how tall a bookshelf do you need. If you can't reach the books, then what is the point? You might need small book racks if you are still building your book collection. Or you don't have too many things to put on your bookshelves. That's fine. Tall or short, there is a bookshelf for you.

Do you need a traditional bookshelf with shelves and a back? These types can be placed anywhere in your home. The back and the sides will keep all your books from falling out. There are open bookshelves also. There might not be a solid back, solid sides, or both. But if that works, it's fine since IKEA has them all

Do you need one shelf on a particular wall? Then a floating shelf is your answer. They can be fixed on any wall and display your things. More than one floating shelf can be put on a wall. You can fix the floating shelves in a pattern on your wall so your room is more lively. After all, your home is a reflection of your personality so let it shine and sparkle.

Shelves for your working area

Organisation is a key factor in your home and office. If you keep things in their correct place, then things won't get lost. This is especially true in an office. You work hard and depend on a lot of things to make sure all your work is complete and correct. You work hard to succeed in life and having an organised office is helpful. That's why having a shelving unit should be on your office shopping list. These units are great for storing important files, extra office supplies, your office bag, or other things.

Don't worry about your home office. IKEA has that covered too. The same kind of shelving units can be put in your office as well as your home office. Your important documents, electronic devices, and books deserve a place and that's where these shelves from IKEA come into play. Everything you need to be productive will be within your arm's reach. These shelves for books can be decorated if you want to brighten up your work area. Just because it is your work area, doesn't mean it can't look colourful and beautiful.

There are spacious cabinets made by IKEA. You can place your most important files and documents inside so they are hidden from prying eyes. The stylish design will match your home decor and you can never have too many storage units. All your things should have their own space and be kept with care. That is why you should buy your furniture from IKEA. You get practicality and stylishness, all in one.
